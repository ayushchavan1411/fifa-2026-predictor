# User Manual – FIFA World Cup 2026 Predictor

## How to Use

Select a mode from the sidebar:

- **World Cup 2026** – view matches and group standings.
- **Player Stats** – search for any player in the tournament.
- **Team Stats** – see full team performance table.
- **Top Scorers** – leaderboard of goal scorers.
- **Match Predictor** – pick two teams to get a detailed win probability.
- **AI Insights & Chat** – ask questions about the World Cup.

## AI Features
- Choose AI provider: ADK Agent, Ollama, or OpenAI BYOK.
- Click "Test Ollama Connection" to verify local setup.
- In Match Predictor, click "AI Analysis of this Match" for a written prediction.

## Language
Switch between English, Hindi, and Telugu using the sidebar dropdown.

## Auto-refresh
Enable "Auto-refresh live scores" to update matches every 30 seconds.
